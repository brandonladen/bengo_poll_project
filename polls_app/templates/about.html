About the Project

This project, VoteNow, is an online voting platform designed to facilitate secure and transparent elections. The platform enables users to register, view candidates, cast their votes, and monitor the results in real-time.

The goal of this platform is to modernize the voting process, making it more accessible, efficient, and user-friendly. It also promotes engagement by providing voters with key information about candidates and their manifestos, helping them make informed decisions.

Project by

Shelomith, a dedicated student at Kibabii University, Kenya, developed this platform as part of a school project. Her commitment to innovation and passion for technology has driven the creation of this secure, user-friendly voting solution.

Shelomith's work demonstrates a commitment to making a positive impact through technology, solving real-world problems in the electoral process. This platform is a testament to the potential of young innovators in Kenya and beyond.
